Platform Rebuilt.
Results Delivered.

Took FundedNext from a fragmented, outdated dashboard to a cohesive modern platform serving 10,000+ traders — cutting workflow inefficiency by 40%, lifting user engagement by 35%, and eliminating 18.5% of support volume through eight targeted product decisions.

RoleProduct Manager II
CompanyNext Ventures · FundedNext
TimelineApril 2025 — Present
Team20 People (Cross-functional)
DomainFintech · SaaS · Prop Trading
The Challenge

A fragmented platform creating real business risk

FundedNext had three compounding problems: an outdated dashboard driving user frustration, zero real-time risk transparency for traders, and compliance gaps that exposed the platform to regulatory and financial risk.

Friction-heavy flows around KYC, payouts, and a forced 2FA onboarding were generating avoidable support volume and damaging early user retention. There was also no behavioral incentive layer — traders weren't engaging with key platform resources or features beyond their core account activity.

The challenge was systemic. This wasn't a list of features to ship — it required a unified product strategy that addressed compliance, UX, automation, and user motivation simultaneously.

My Approach

Eight decisions that moved the platform forward

01

Operational workflows were entirely manual, creating bottlenecks across the team. Architected a full automation agent in n8n with a 20-person cross-functional team — delivering a 40%+ gain in workflow efficiency.

02

The platform had no structured notification layer, leaving traders disconnected from key account events. Shipped a full notification system (backend and frontend) across both CFD and Futures products — lifting user engagement by 35%.

03

An outdated dashboard was creating friction at every critical touchpoint — KYC verification, payouts, and account management. Leading the full dashboard revamp, modernizing flows to reduce drop-off and lower support contact at key stages.

04

Traders had no visibility into their real-time risk exposure, making it impossible to self-correct before breaching limits. Introduced Risk Cards on the Account Overview — giving traders a live view of their risk profile and the direct impact of their trading behavior.

05

Abusive trading behavior was going undetected, creating platform integrity and financial exposure. Built a compliance regulations framework with active monitoring of flagged accounts and enforcement protocols for non-compliant traders.

06

FAQ engagement was passive and plan purchases weren't being driven by in-product moments. Created FNHunt — a gamified coupon-code hunt embedded in the FAQ, turning passive browsing into an active discovery mechanism that directly drove plan purchases.

07

Customer pain points were distributed across the journey with no single owner accountable for the full experience. Built and led the Identity & Investigation Squad — a dedicated cross-functional unit owning end-to-end customer journey diagnosis and continuous improvement.

08

Forcing all users into 2FA at signup was creating onboarding friction and flooding support with avoidable tickets. Redesigned the 2FA flow: removed it from free user onboarding entirely, enforced it for paid users post-KYC or after 14 days — resolving 18.5% of all support tickets.
