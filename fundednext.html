The Challenge

A platform in need of a full rethink

FundedNext needed a holistic product evolution. The dashboard was outdated with scattered notifications and critical gaps in compliance enforcement. Risk transparency was missing — traders couldn't understand their exposure in real time.

Customer journey pain points around KYC, payouts, and a rigid 2FA flow were driving a significant share of support tickets. The platform also lacked mechanisms to incentivize user engagement with key resources, creating a gap between the product and its users.

The challenge wasn't one problem — it was an entire product that needed a coherent strategy pulling all these threads together into a unified vision.

My Approach

Leading end-to-end transformation

01

Led a 20-person cross-functional team to architect an end-to-end automation agent using n8n — improving workflow efficiency by 40%+

02

Shipped a notification system (backend & frontend) for both CFD and Futures — driving a 35% engagement increase

03

Leading the full dashboard revamp — modernizing the UI/UX to reduce customer pain points during KYC verification and Rewards (payout) flows

04

Introduced Risk Cards to Account Overview so traders see real-time risk exposure and understand the repercussions of their trading behavior

05

Built a compliance regulations framework for abusive users — heavily monitoring flagged accounts and banning non-compliant traders to protect platform integrity

06

Created FNHunt — a gamified coupon-code hunt embedded in the FAQ, substantially increasing FAQ engagement and plan purchases through single-use discoverable codes

07

Lead of the Identity & Investigation Squad — identifying customer journey pain points and systematically improving them across the full platform

08

Redesigned the 2FA flow — free users no longer forced into 2FA (removing onboarding friction), paid users enforced after 14 days or post-KYC, resolving 18.5% of support tickets
