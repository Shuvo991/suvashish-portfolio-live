Education Cannot Wait LMS

A learning management system that brought structured, accessible education to 2,000+ crisis-affected students worldwide — bridging the gap where traditional schooling couldn't reach.

RoleBusiness Analyst / PM
CompanySave the Children International
Timeline2022 — 2025
Team15 People (Cross-functional)
DomainEdTech · Global Impact · Humanitarian
The Challenge

Education where systems had broken down

Students in crisis-affected regions lacked access to structured education. Existing tools were fragmented, and stakeholders across multiple countries had competing priorities and technical constraints.

The platform needed to be robust enough for international stakeholders managing diverse curricula, yet accessible enough for students in areas with limited connectivity. A single implementation had to work across wildly different contexts.

Beyond the technology, the challenge was aligning a 15-person cross-functional team spanning education specialists, IT engineers, humanitarian field staff, and international donors — all with different definitions of success.

My Approach

Agile delivery across complex stakeholder terrain

01

Led a 15-person cross-functional team through requirements gathering with international stakeholders spanning education, IT, and humanitarian domains

02

Aligned system features with diverse stakeholder goals — balancing educator needs, donor requirements, and student access across multiple contexts

03

Ensured seamless vendor integration while maintaining quality standards through Agile practices and regular sprint reviews

04

Built feedback loops with end users — including teachers and students in crisis zones — to continuously improve the learning experience

05

Implemented interactive radio content and offline-capable learning modules to reach students with low or no internet connectivity
