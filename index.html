FundedNext Automation & Notifications

Building an AI-powered automation agent and notification system for a global fintech platform

40%+ Workflow Efficiency Gain
n8n Automation SaaS Fintech Notifications

My Approach

Save the Children International 2022 — 2023

Learning Tree Global LMS

A multi-country learning platform reaching 1,200+ children across 5 nations

1,200+ Children Reached Across 5 Countries
Multi-country Localization EdTech Usability Impact

The Challenge

Teachers and caregivers in Guatemala, Colombia, Ukraine, Poland, and Pakistan needed a unified yet locally adaptable learning platform. Language barriers, infrastructure differences, and varying educational standards made this a complex product challenge.

My Approach

  1. Led product development coordinating across 5 country teams with different languages and curricula
  2. Adapted the platform to regional needs — localized content, offline-capable modules, and culturally relevant UX
  3. Implemented Agile ceremonies adapted for async, cross-timezone collaboration
  4. Built analytics to measure engagement and iterate on content effectiveness per region

The Outcome

Reached 1,200+ children with a 30% increase in engagement during early adoption. The platform's modular architecture became the blueprint for subsequent Save the Children LMS projects worldwide.

AI-Powered Enterprise Innovation
