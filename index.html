FundedNext — Platform Evolution & Product Leadership

Leading a full-scope product transformation across automation, dashboard revamp, compliance, risk visibility, and user engagement for a global prop-trading platform

The Challenge

FundedNext needed a holistic product evolution — from an outdated dashboard and scattered notifications to critical gaps in compliance enforcement, risk transparency, and customer journey pain points around KYC, payouts, and a rigid 2FA flow that was driving a significant share of support tickets. The platform also lacked mechanisms to incentivize user engagement with knowledge resources.

My Approach

  1. Led a 20-person cross-functional team to architect an end-to-end automation agent using n8n, improving workflow efficiency by 40%+
  2. Shipped a notification system (backend & frontend) for both CFD and Futures, driving 35% engagement increase
  3. Leading the full dashboard revamp — modernizing the UI/UX to reduce customer pain points during KYC verification and Rewards (payout) flows
  4. Introduced Risk Cards to Account Overview so traders see real-time risk exposure and understand the repercussions of their trading behavior
  5. Built a regulations framework for abusive users — heavily monitoring flagged accounts and banning non-compliant traders to protect platform integrity
  6. Created FNHunt — a gamified coupon-code hunt embedded in the FAQ, substantially increasing FAQ engagement and plan purchases through single-use discoverable codes
  7. Lead of the Identity & Investigation Squad — identifying customer journey pain points and systematically improving them
  8. Redesigned the 2FA flow — free users are no longer forced into 2FA (removing a major onboarding friction), while paid users are enforced 2FA after 14 days or post-KYC verification to secure profiles before payout, resolving 18.5% of support tickets

The Outcome

Transformed FundedNext from a feature-by-feature product into a cohesive, modern platform. The automation agent cut manual work by 40%+, the 2FA redesign eliminated 18.5% of support tickets, FNHunt drove measurable spikes in FAQ engagement and plan purchases, Risk Cards improved trader awareness, and the compliance framework strengthened platform trust. The dashboard revamp is creating a fundamentally better experience for KYC, payouts, and day-to-day trading.
