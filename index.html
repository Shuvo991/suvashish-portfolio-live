How I Build Products

I start with the outcome and work backwards — through data, design, and engineering — until the product earns its place in the roadmap.

01

Discover

Deep user research, data analysis, and stakeholder alignment to understand the real problem before touching a solution.

02

Define

Precise PRDs, OKRs, and success metrics. Ruthless prioritization with the WHY always visible to the team.

03

Build

Cross-functional coordination, sprint management, and clear tradeoff decisions that keep the team aligned and shipping fast.

04

Iterate

Analytics reviews, A/B testing, retrospectives, and continuous product evolution driven by real user data and outcomes.
